## Executive Summary
This analysis examined weekly sales performance across Walmart stores from 31-12-2010 to 01-04-2011 identify key revenue drivers and performance gaps.
Key findings:
- Sales are significantly higher during holiday weeks, with some stores experiencing over 19.77% uplift.
- Holiday sensitivity is uneven: certain stores benefit strongly, while some others show decrease in sales.
Recommendations:
- Increase inventory and staffing for high holiday-sensitive stores.
- Investigate underperforming stores with weak holiday response for pricing or local marketing issues.
- Use sales tiering to allocate resources more efficiently across stores.
