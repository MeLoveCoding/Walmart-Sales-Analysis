# Walmart Weekly Sales Analysis

## Business Problem
Walmart headquarters wants to understand how store performance varies across locations and how external factors such as holidays and economic conditions affect weekly sales. The goal is to identify high- and low-performing stores and recommend data-driven strategies for improving overall performance.

## Datasets
The dataset used in this analysis is available online, at: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mikhail1681/walmart-sales.
The dataset contains weekly sales data for 45 Walmart stores, including holiday indicators and economic variables such as temperature, fuel price, CPI, and unemployment rate.

## Analysis Objectives 
- Evaluate store-level sales performance
- Measure the impact of holidays on sales
- Analyze relationships between sales and external economic factors
- Segment stores into performance tiers

## Tools and Skill Used
- SQL (MySQL): aggregation, window functions, CTEs
- Data analysis & business interpretation

## Key Insights
- Holidays usually improve the overall sales of every stores, however the impact varies differently between each stores
- Other than holidays, other factors like tempreature, fuel price, CPI and unemployment rate does not have a considerably impact on overall sales
- Indicated the most efficiently operated stores based on Sales Quantiatives 

## Recommendations
- Prioritize high-performing stores for growth initiatives.
- Use targeted interventions for underperforming stores.
